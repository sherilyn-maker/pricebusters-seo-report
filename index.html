Google AI Overview — The Hidden AI Channel

Most Google AI Overview traffic is invisible in GA4 today

Google AI Overview (the AI-generated answer boxes at the top of search results) currently routes clicks as standard google / organic sessions. Google does not yet pass a separate UTM parameter for AI Overview clicks. This means Price Busters' 1,008 Google organic sessions contain an unknown proportion from AI Overview citations — potentially 10–30% based on industry estimates for plumbing-related searches where AI Overviews are common.

What triggers AI Overview appearances:

  • FAQPage schema — Google's AI pulls FAQ Q&A pairs directly into answers
  • Deep, authoritative content answering specific questions
  • Strong local business schema with accurate NAP data
  • High aggregate review scores (AggregateRating schema)
  • Content that directly answers "how much does X cost" and "how long does X take"

Price Busters' current AI overview status:

  • Strong FAQPage schema on sewer repair and water heater pages
